Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the benefits of taking the Olympiads?

    It helps you to:
    Increase your knowledge,
    Strengthen your Resume,
    Stay updated on the current trends,
    Preparing for your placements,
    Preparing for any other type of competitive exams,
    Enhance your knowledge and skills.
    Also, Participants receive state and national level ranking along with Prizes that include cash awards, medals, gifts, certificates etc.

  • Who is eligible to participate?

    Any student currently studying in class 3 to 12, graduated or graduating, working professional or any competitive exam aspirant is eligible to take the exam.

  • What is the syllabus for the Olympiads?

    For students of class 3 to 12, the syllabus of the Olympiads is the same as followed by the CBSE, ICSE and various state boards.
    For Grads and Finance Olympiad, the syllabus is specific to the Exam you choose to register for. You can visit the respective olympiad pages to know more about the syllabus.

  • Do I get a certificate of participation?

    Yes, Certificates are provided to all the participant along with various cash prizes, medals, merit certificates to the top rankers!

  • What are the resources/books I can use to prepare?

    For students of class 3 to 12, no special preparation is required. All olympiads are designed in such a way that anyone can comfortably participate just by studying their school syllabus. For the finance olympiad, you'll need to be updated with personal finance trends.
    For Grads Olympiad too, no special preparation is required. It is based on reasoning, aptitude and grammar.

  • What is the mode of conduction of the exam?

    All Exams are conducted completely in online mode

  • In which language the Olympiads are conducted in?

    All Exams are conducted in the English language.

  • Can I take part in more than 1 olympiads?

    All students currently studying in standards 3 to 12 can participate in any 1 or 2 or 3 or all the 4 olympiads:
    Springfield International Science Olympiad (SISO)
    Springfield International Mathematics Olympiad (SIMO)
    Springfield International English Olympiad (SIEO)
    Springfield International Cyber Olympiad (SICO)
    While Graduates, Undergraduates and Working Professionals can participate in any of the following two olympiads or both :
    Grads Olympiad, and
    Finance Olympiad.

  • On which days usually are the olympiads conducted?

    All the Exams are usually conducted on Sundays.

  • How will I know about all the details like result declaration, etc once I register?

    You can like our page on Facebook(fb.com/sfoly) or Instagram(instagram.com/sfolym) to be in touch.
    We will also mail you for necessary updates like Result Declaration, etc.

  • Is it necessary to have a desktop/computer to participate?

    No, the exam can be taken from a mobile device as well!
